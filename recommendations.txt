Disease Name: Bacterial Spot (Tomato)

Severity Level:
Moderate to Severe (spreads rapidly under warm, wet conditions)

Recommended Treatment:

Remove and destroy heavily infected leaves or plants

Apply copper-based bactericides (copper hydroxide or copper oxychloride)

Use copper + mancozeb for better control

Apply biological agents such as Bacillus subtilis where available

Avoid overhead irrigation and improve field sanitation

Do not exceed recommended chemical dosage

Avoid spraying during windy conditions

Wash hands and equipment after application
